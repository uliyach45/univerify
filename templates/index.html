Core Capabilities

Everything you need to secure
institutional documents

A complete cryptographic document management system — no third-party dependency, no subscription fees.

Mutual TLS Authentication
X.509 client certificates establish a cryptographically verifiable identity for every signer. No password-based vulnerabilities.
SHA-256 Blockchain
Every signature is recorded as an immutable block. Hash chaining ensures the audit trail is tamper-evident and verifiable by any party.
Tamper Detection
Any unauthorized modification is instantly detected via hash mismatch. Original signer, timestamp, and fingerprint are displayed as proof.
Email Token Authorization
Legitimate document updates require a time-limited cryptographic token delivered via SMTP over TLS.
Public Verification
Any third party can verify a document's authenticity by uploading it. No account required.
Full Audit Logs
Every action — login, signing, update, tamper attempt — is logged in real time with timestamps and user identifiers.
How It Works

Four phases, complete protection

From initial signing to tamper detection, every interaction is cryptographically secured and permanently recorded.

Upload & Sign
Upload your document. mTLS handshake extracts your X.509 certificate. SHA-256 hash is computed and signed.
Blockchain Record
A new block containing the hash, certificate fingerprint, email, signature, and timestamp is appended to the chain.
Authorized Update
Legitimate revisions require a 5-minute email token. A new version block is appended with parent_hash linking to the original.
Instant Verification
Any party uploads the file. System queries the blockchain and returns signer identity, timestamp, and version history.
